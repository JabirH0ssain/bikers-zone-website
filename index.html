Frequently Asked Questions

What are the most important things I should know about riding a bike?

Safety First! Always obey the rules of the road. Obey all traffic signals, signs, and laws. Get in the mindset of “driving” your bike—not just “riding” your bike. This will help you be a more focused and legally compliant bike rider.

How can I tell if my helmet is old and I need a new one?

1. Basically if your helmet is older than 5 years
2. If the interior have started to come off.
3. If the exterior is showing signs of wear & tear.
4. You can't get the stink off.
5. If he helmet wobbles

My bike has been in storage is it safe to ride?

It depends on how many days or months the bike has been in storage. But if the condition looks bad or the bike doesn't starts easily, then you should visit your nearest service center.

What rules should I follow when riding my bike?

1. At first, take a safety course.
2. Wear motorcycle gear
3. Inspect your motorbike before each ride.
4. Obey traffic rules, Use signals and drive the speed limit.
5. Be visible 6. stay at a safe distance.
7. Check the weather before going on a ride.
8. Be observant and watch for road hazards.
